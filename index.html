Who Are We

Warwick Climbing Club is, unsurprisingly, based in the Warwickshire County Town of Warwick, we meet twice a week [Wednesday and Thursday] at our local climbing walls with a post climbing pub meet on the Wednesday

Our Climbing Wall

  • Where Is The Wall
  • When Is It Open
  • Who Can Climb
  • What is the Cost

The Climbing Wall is sited within the St Nicholas Park Leisure Center in Warwick

The Wall is open on a Wednesday Evening between 07:00 and 22:00

The wall is for the exclusive use of Warwick Climbing Club Members

The cost is £5 per session or £4.40 if a block of 5 is purchased in advance
